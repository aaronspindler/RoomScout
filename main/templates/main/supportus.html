Support Us

Roomscout.ca is a project that was started to help students just like myself with managing expenses with roommates while keeping relationships strong. Roomscout has always been free, and I would love to keep it that way. What we ask for in return is that some people who can afford it pay what they think is fair for this service.

We have tried to develop Roomscout.ca with a very tight budget and bootstrapping the company, however there are some things that we just can't get around.
